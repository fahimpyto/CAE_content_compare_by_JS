LET'S TALK

Frequently
Asked Questions

What’s a full-service digital agency?

It’s one team handling design, development, and marketing. Fewer vendors, fewer handoffs, more control.

What makes Linkup ST different?

We think across functions. Designers understand dev. Developers know what marketing needs. Everyone’s aligned. That saves time, money, and a ton of frustration. As a full- service digital agency and a trusted growth agency, we deliver unified strategy and execution across every digital touchpoint.

Who do you usually work with?

Mostly small and mid-sized teams — service businesses, retail, health, SaaS. Companies with vision but not always full internal teams.

What design work do you cover?

Everything from UI/UX and redesigns to audits and prototypes. It’s not about making it pretty — it’s about making it work.

What kind of software do you build?

We build things that run your business smoother. Platforms, internal tools, mobile apps, AI automations and agents — the good stuff that saves time and scales ops.

How do you approach marketing?

We don’t sell buzzwords. We build solid strategy and execution: SEO, content, emails, campaigns, video. Clear offers, strong funnels, measurable growth. As a digital growth agency, we focus on visibility and conversion across every step of your customer journey.
